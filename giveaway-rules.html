Giveaway Rules

These rules govern all giveaways organized by SuperStacked Giveaways.

1. Eligibility

  • Participants must be 18 years or older.
  • Open to residents where participation is legally allowed.

2. Entry Method

  • Each entry requires a payment of GHS 75 via Paystack.
  • After payment, a unique entry code will be issued.

3. Winner Selection

  • Winners are selected randomly.
  • Results are announced via email and Instagram (@Stacked.giveaway).

4. Prizes

Prizes may include items such as smartphones, gaming consoles, or cash equivalents.

5. No Guarantee

Purchasing a ticket does not guarantee winning.

6. Modification

SuperStacked Giveaways reserves the right to modify these rules at any time.

7. Support & Enquiries

For any questions regarding giveaway rules or participation, please contact our technical support team at stackedgiveaway225@gmail.com .
